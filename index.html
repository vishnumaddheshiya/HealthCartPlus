Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days within Kolkata and 4-7 days for other cities in India.

Do I need a prescription for all medicines?

No, only prescription drugs require a valid prescription. Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines can be purchased without a prescription.

Can I return medicines?

Due to safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on medicines once they have been delivered. However, if you receive damaged or incorrect products, please contact our support team immediately.
